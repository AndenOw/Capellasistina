The Exhibit Focus

We are producers of best-in-class immersive, VR, and AR events worldwide. We design, build, and operate experiential exhibition spaces supported by cutting-edge technology and AI to maximize the visitor experience. Through Global Einstein, LLC in cooperation with the Hebrew University of Jerusalem we will deliver an extraordinary exhibition experience that brings the monumental life and works of Dr. Albert Einstein to visitors of all ages around the world. A brilliant scientist, thoughtful philosopher, dedicated educator, and caring humanist, Dr. Einstein’s enduring contributions continue to shape the way we view our universe and ourselves

People are eager for immersive, educational, and entertaining experiences that create lasting memories. A touring exhibition that brings the groundbreaking accomplishments of Einstein to life in fun and engaging ways will connect visitors to historic moments, inspire the next generation of scientists, and empower visitors to challenge their perceptions of the world through science. We promise a revolutionary exhibition experience that makes Einstein’s achievements personal, approachable, and fun. We invite you to invest with us in this innovative new exhibition.

We envision more than just an exhibition, but an entire ecosystem of experiences and partnerships that support one another in the pursuit of shaping Einstein’s worldwide legacy. We will develop films, educational materials, special events, guest speakers, and merchandise to support the primary exhibition experience.

Project Vision

Albert Einstein’s monumental life and works persistently resonate in scientific and academic communities and inspire the world as a popular cultural icon. A brilliant scientist, thoughtful philosopher, dedicated educator, and caring humanist, Professor Einstein’s enduring contributions continue to shape the way we view our universe and ourselves.

Impact Exhibit

Large-scale , powerful imagery can quickly communicate ideas and make a lasting impression on audiences. Awe inspiring -Information can be beautiful: attract, surprise, then educate. Witness the Eddington Eclipse Experiment that marked the beginning of modern physics. Einstein ’s famous equation –E=mc2, and fusion in action through media of the sun.

Our Audience

Dr. Einstein is routinely in the top five of the most recognizable people of all time. We will target museum visitors who expect information and entertainment. We will attract families with children of all ages. We will focus on students and educators as we promote Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
