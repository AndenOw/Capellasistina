The Exhibit Focus

Welcome to Global Exhibitions & Events, LLC (Global), where we specialize in creating unparalleled immersive experiences that transcend time and space. With a dedicated team of seasoned exhibition veterans hailing from various disciplines, including management, design, technology, and archiving, we are committed to delivering unforgettable encounters with the world's most iconic cultural treasures.

Embark on a journey with us as we unveil our ambitious plan to bring Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel to North America. Through a strategic partnership with the Vatican Museum and Scripta Maneant, our exclusive publishing partner, we have gained access to a treasure trove of Renaissance art and artifacts. Utilizing cutting-edge technology and high-resolution imagery, we invite you to experience the Sistine Chapel as never before – up close and personal, revealing the intricate details of Michelangelo's masterpieces.

Our mission is to promote the artistic, historical, and spiritual significance of this singular wonder of the art world through a comprehensive North American tour. From the majesty of the ceiling adorned with the Creation of Adam to the awe-inspiring Last Judgment, our exhibition promises to immerse you in the timeless beauty and profound meaning of Michelangelo's work.

Accredited investors are invited to explore our detailed development process and financial information, available upon request. Join us in shaping the future of immersive cultural experiences as we embark on this extraordinary journey to bring the Sistine Chapel to audiences across North America.

Project Vision

Albert Einstein’s monumental life and works persistently resonate in scientific and academic communities and inspire the world as a popular cultural icon. A brilliant scientist, thoughtful philosopher, dedicated educator, and caring humanist, Professor Einstein’s enduring contributions continue to shape the way we view our universe and ourselves.

Impact Exhibit

Large-scale , powerful imagery can quickly communicate ideas and make a lasting impression on audiences. Awe inspiring -Information can be beautiful: attract, surprise, then educate. Witness the Eddington Eclipse Experiment that marked the beginning of modern physics. Einstein ’s famous equation –E=mc2, and fusion in action through media of the sun.

Our Audience

Dr. Einstein is routinely in the top five of the most recognizable people of all time. We will target museum visitors who expect information and entertainment. We will attract families with children of all ages. We will focus on students and educators as we promote Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
