Who We Are

Over 100 Years of Experience in the Entertainment Industry

The entertainment sector is wide and varied— encompassing everything from concerts to performances to festivals, which all require a slightly different set of skills and expertise.

Touring exhibitions are unlike many other market segments—with long runs, daily daytime visitation, a high volume of inventory, and a specific set of visitor expectations.

Our team of experts has learned from years of industry experience, constantly adapting and innovating with a continued commitment to high-quality, unforgettable experiences.

Our Partners the Vatican Museum and Scripta Maneant Editore

The company has partnered with the Vatican Museum and their exclusive publishing partner, Scripta Maneant, who hold the rights to a vast archive of Renaissance art and artifacts. These include a five year photographic project that has captured the Sistine Chapel through high resolution imagery, allowing viewers to get up close and personal with the art of Michelangelo as never before seen.

Sistine Chapel Tour Planning
